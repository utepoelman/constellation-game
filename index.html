Game Rules

Objective

Claim nodes on the constellation board by creating matching numbers through mathematical operations.

Gameplay

  • Each turn, you get 3 random dice numbers (1-6)
  • Combine two numbers with an operation (+, -, Ã—, Ã·)
  • Then combine the result with the third number using another operation
  • The final result must be an integer
  • Click any unclaimed node with that number to claim it for your color

Scoring

  • Nodes form clusters when adjacent (connected by lines)
  • Your score is the sum of squares of cluster sizes
  • Example: A cluster of 3 nodes = 3Â² = 9 points
  • Two clusters of 2 nodes = 2Â² + 2Â² = 8 points
  • Larger clusters give significantly higher scores!

Winning

The game ends when all nodes are claimed, or when a player requests finish and the opponent completes their turn. Highest score wins!
